Who I Am

About Me

Over fifteen years at Verizon, I have led enterprise AI transformation — founding the AI Center of Excellence, scaling the AI and data science function to 100+ engineers, and building production-grade GenAI and agentic AI platforms in direct collaboration with LangChain's engineering team.

I architected document intelligence infrastructure processing 200TB+ of enterprise content and established AI governance frameworks covering full model lifecycle visibility, drift and bias monitoring, and audit-defensible risk controls.

My technical foundation spans the complete AI and data stack: multi-agent orchestration, RAG pipelines, MLOps/LLMOps, petabyte-scale data lakehouse and streaming architectures, and cloud-native AI platforms across AWS and GCP.

What drives me is building things that last — platforms teams actually use, governance frameworks that hold up under audit, and engineering cultures where AI is treated as a discipline rather than a trend.
